Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Professional teeth whitening at our clinic is completely safe and largely painless. Some patients may experience mild sensitivity for 24–48 hours after treatment, which subsides naturally. We use carefully calibrated professional-grade agents that protect your enamel throughout the process.
Dental implants are designed to last a lifetime with proper care. The titanium implant post itself can last 20–25+ years, while the crown (the visible tooth) typically lasts 10–15 years before it may need replacement. Regular brushing, flossing, and dental check-ups are key to implant longevity.
For most patients, we recommend a dental check-up and professional cleaning every 6 months. Patients with specific conditions like gum disease, a history of cavities, or ongoing orthodontic treatment may need more frequent visits. Regular appointments allow us to catch issues early, saving you time, discomfort, and cost.
Braces are not painful when they are first placed, however you may feel soreness or pressure for 2–4 days after each adjustment appointment as your teeth begin to shift. This is completely normal and can be managed with over-the-counter pain relief. Modern bracket designs are much more comfortable than older versions, and the results are well worth it.
A smile makeover is a personalised combination of cosmetic dental procedures — such as teeth whitening, veneers, dental bonding, Invisalign, and gum contouring — designed to transform the appearance of your smile. Dr. Aanchal conducts a detailed consultation to understand your goals and creates a bespoke treatment plan to achieve your dream smile.
