Frequently Asked Questions

You should visit a general physician for any non-emergency health concerns, persistent symptoms like fever, cough, or pain, and for management of chronic conditions like diabetes or BP. Regular checkups are also recommended to monitor your overall health and catch potential issues early.
Symptoms like high-grade fever, chest pain, difficulty breathing, severe abdominal pain, or suspected poisoning/snake bite require immediate medical attention. Dr. Aanchal's clinic is equipped to handle such emergencies with priority care.
For healthy adults, an annual comprehensive health checkup is usually sufficient. However, if you have chronic conditions like diabetes or hypertension, more frequent visits (every 3-6 months) may be necessary to monitor and adjust your treatment plan.
