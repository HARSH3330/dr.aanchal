Dental Implants

Restorative Dentistry

Dental Implants

Dental implants are the gold standard for replacing missing teeth. A titanium post is surgically placed into the jawbone, acting as an artificial root. A custom crown is then attached, giving you a natural-looking, permanent tooth that functions exactly like the real thing.

Permanent, lifetime solution
Natural look and feel
Preserves jawbone structure
No impact on adjacent teeth

Root Canal Treatment

Endodontics

Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment removes infected pulp tissue from inside your tooth, eliminating pain and infection while saving your natural tooth. With modern anaesthesia and rotary endodontic instruments, the procedure is virtually painless and often completed in one visit.

Saves the natural tooth
Modern rotary endodontic technique
Often completed in a single visit
Post-procedure crown placement included

Invisalign

Preventive Care

Dental Cleaning
