Our Doctor

Dedicated to Your Dental Health

Dr. Aanchal is a highly accomplished cosmetic and general dentist with over 10 years of clinical experience. She completed her BDS with distinction and went on to earn an MDS in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery, followed by a prestigious Fellowship in Cosmetic Dentistry.

Her philosophy is simple: every patient deserves personalised, compassionate care in a comfortable environment. She continuously updates her skills with the latest advancements in dental technology to ensure her patients receive world-class treatment.

Outside the clinic, Dr. Aanchal is a passionate dental educator, regularly conducting workshops and awareness programmes on oral hygiene in the community.
